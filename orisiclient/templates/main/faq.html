0. What is Orisi

Orisi is an idea of Distributed Oracle system, read more at Orisi.org This site has been built for educational purposes.

1. What is Timelock

Timelock smart contract works like this: You send money to multisig address, it stays there for the time(minutes) defined by you and get back to you on right after this specified time.

It would be just a simple web service but the difference here is that you transaction is being signed by independent oracles only after they agree that the time has passed. Those Oracles are hidden in the safe locations, without access to the public internet besides Fastcast boradcasting service that handles communication between clients and oracles.

You can connect to see ALL THE MESSAGES that are being sent through the Fastcast network. or fetch them using REST API and verify how it all works.

You are not meant to earn anything, in fact you will probably loose ~1mBTC just to try it.

7. For how long should I set the timelock?

Setting timelock time for less than 15 minutes is probably ineffective, the fastest time we have achieved with the whole contract to finish was 16 minutes(timelock = "1 minute"). For now do not set it to more than 48 hours. We reserve a right to take down this site with 1 week notice.
